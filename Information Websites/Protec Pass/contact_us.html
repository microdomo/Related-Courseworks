FAQs

What is a password manager?
A password manager, just like its name, manages your password for you. It is a software application that helps manage credentials online and in turn give you greater convenience online.
Can Protec Pass see our passwords saved?
No. Protec Pass is designed such that your data are encrypted and decrypted on your own end. They can only be access using a passsword that only you know about. Hence, Protec Pass will not be able to see your passwords.
What is a password generator?
A password generator helps generate random passwords for you. These passwords contains alphabets, numbers and special characters which makes them stronger and safer.
